About

The player & the instrument

Portrait — replace with real photo

Hayoung Lee is a Korean-born classical guitarist based in Alicante, Spain.

Her playing is shaped by the warmth of the Spanish Mediterranean and the quiet lyricism of her Korean roots — a single voice moving between two homes.

She studied at Seoul National University and the Conservatorio Superior de Música de Madrid, and now performs across Spain and Korea.
